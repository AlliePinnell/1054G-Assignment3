Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

89 year old Classic Candy! Invented by Harry Burnett 'H. B.' Reese, working for the Hershey Company in the early 1900's. Reese began making candies in his home, and by 1923 he created the H. B. Reese Candy Company. In 1928, Reese invented Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, which were coated in Hershey chocolate.

Always Time to Eat Reese! Reese’s makes enough Peanut Butter Cups in one year to feed one cup to every person in the USA, Japan, Europe, Australia, China, Africa & India. That’s about 25 million cups a day!

Tons of types and styles! There have been over 20 variations of the Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup, that had been sold as limited edition cups for various occasions, such as; Christmas, Halloween & Valentine’s Day. There are also many other kinds of candies based on the Reese's Peanut Butter Cup, such as; Reese's Pieces, Reese's Sticks, Reese's Fast Break, Reese's Big Cup, Reese's Nutrageous, Reese's Crispy Crunchy Bar, and many more.
